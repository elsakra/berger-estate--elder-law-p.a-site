Great contracts do two things: they set expectations when everyone is aligned, and they allocate risk when they're not.

Three clauses that matter more than most people think:

- **Scope and acceptance**: define what "done" means.
- **Termination**: clarify off-ramps and fees.
- **Dispute resolution**: pick venue and process before emotions decide.
